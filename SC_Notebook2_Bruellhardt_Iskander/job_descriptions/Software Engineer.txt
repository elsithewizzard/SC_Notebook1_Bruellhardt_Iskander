# Job Title: Software Engineer
**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]  
**Department:** Engineering  
**Reports to:** Engineering Manager

## Job Summary
We are looking for a passionate and skilled Software Engineer to join our growing engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality software solutions that solve complex problems for our users. You will work closely with product managers, designers, and other engineers to build scalable, secure, and efficient applications.

## Key Responsibilities
*   **Development:** Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code in accordance with company standards.
*   **Collaboration:** Participate in the full software development lifecycle (SDLC), including requirements gathering, design, coding, testing, and deployment.
*   **Architecture:** Contribute to architectural discussions and help make decisions regarding technologies and patterns.
*   **Code Quality:** Conduct and participate in peer code reviews to ensure code quality and knowledge sharing.
*   **Maintenance:** Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing software systems to improve performance and reliability.
*   **Documentation:** Create and maintain technical documentation for features, APIs, and system designs.
*   **Continuous Improvement:** Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies to ensure our tech stack remains modern and competitive.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent professional experience).
*   **Experience:** 3+ years of professional software development experience.
*   **Proficiency:** Strong command of at least one major programming language (e.g., Python, Java, JavaScript/TypeScript, Go, or C#).
*   **Front-End/Back-End:** Experience with modern frameworks (e.g., React, Vue, Node.js, Django, or Spring Boot).
*   **Databases:** Solid understanding of relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and/or NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Redis).
*   **Tools:** Proficient with Git version control and CI/CD pipelines.
*   **Problem Solving:** Strong analytical skills and the ability to break down complex requirements into manageable tasks.

## Preferred Qualifications
*   Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).
*   Knowledge of containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes).
*   Understanding of microservices architecture.
*   Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment.
*   Contributions to open-source projects or a strong portfolio on GitHub.

## Soft Skills
*   **Communication:** Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
*   **Adaptability:** Comfortable working in a fast-paced, evolving environment.
*   **Teamwork:** A "team-first" mentality with a willingness to mentor junior developers.

### How to customize this for your specific needs:

1.  **For a Senior Role:** Change "3+ years" to "5-8+ years." Add responsibilities like "Mentoring junior staff" and "Leading system design for high-traffic applications."
2.  **For a Junior Role:** Focus more on "Willingness to learn," "Foundational knowledge of CS algorithms," and "Assisting in bug fixes and small feature development."
3.  **For Frontend Focus:** Emphasize HTML/CSS, UI/UX principles, and specific libraries like React or Angular.
4.  **For Backend Focus:** Emphasize API design (REST/GraphQL), database optimization, and server-side logic.
