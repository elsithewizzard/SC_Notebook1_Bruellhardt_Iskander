# Job Title: Automotive Mechanic / Service Technician

**Location:** [Insert City, State]
**Job Type:** [Full-time/Part-time/Contract]
**Experience Level:** [Entry-Level/Mid-Level/Senior]

### Job Summary
We are seeking a skilled and reliable Mechanic to join our automotive team. The successful candidate will be responsible for diagnosing, maintaining, and repairing customer vehicles. Your goal will be to provide excellent service, ensure vehicle safety, and maintain high levels of customer satisfaction. From routine oil changes to complex engine diagnostics, you will play a critical role in keeping our customers on the road.

### Key Responsibilities
*   **Diagnostics:** Use computerized diagnostic tools and physical inspections to identify mechanical and electrical problems in various makes and models.
*   **Maintenance:** Perform routine maintenance tasks, including oil changes, fluid flushes, tire rotations, wheel alignments, and brake inspections.
*   **Repairs:** Repair or replace worn or faulty parts, including engines, transmissions, steering systems, suspensions, braking systems, and exhaust systems.
*   **Testing:** Conduct test drives and use specialized equipment to ensure all repairs are completed correctly and the vehicle is safe for operation.
*   **Documentation:** Maintain accurate records of all work performed, parts used, and time spent on each job using our shop management software.
*   **Communication:** Explain technical issues and necessary repairs to Service Advisors or customers in a clear, easy-to-understand manner.
*   **Safety & Cleanliness:** Adhere to all shop safety protocols and maintain a clean, organized work environment.

### Required Skills & Qualifications
*   **Education:** High School Diploma or GED required. Completion of a post-secondary automotive program or apprenticeship is highly preferred.
*   **Experience:** [Insert Number] years of experience working as a mechanic in a professional shop environment.
*   **Technical Knowledge:** Deep understanding of internal combustion engines, electronic systems, and mechanical components.
*   **Tools:** Must possess a basic set of personal hand tools (specialized power tools and diagnostic equipment will be provided).
*   **Physical Requirements:** Ability to lift up to 50 lbs, stand for long periods, and work in various positions (crawling, kneeling, reaching).
*   **License:** A valid driver’s license and a clean driving record are mandatory.

### Preferred Qualifications
*   **ASE Certifications:** Current ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certifications are a major plus.
*   **Specialization:** Experience with specific brands (e.g., Ford, Toyota, BMW) or specialized systems (e.g., Diesel, Hybrid/EV, Transmissions).
*   **State Inspector License:** [If applicable in your state], a valid State Inspection/Emissions license.

### Core Competencies
*   **Problem-Solving:** Ability to logically troubleshoot complex mechanical issues.
*   **Attention to Detail:** Ensuring every bolt is tightened and every fluid level is checked.
*   **Time Management:** Ability to meet deadlines and manage multiple repair orders efficiently.
*   **Integrity:** Providing honest assessments and only recommending necessary repairs.
