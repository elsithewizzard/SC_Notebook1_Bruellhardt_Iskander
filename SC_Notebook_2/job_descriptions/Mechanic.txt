# Job Description: Automotive Mechanic

**Job Title:** Automotive Mechanic / Service Technician
**Location:** [Insert City, State]
**Job Type:** [Full-Time/Part-Time]
**Reports To:** Shop Manager / Service Manager

### Job Summary
We are seeking a skilled and reliable Mechanic to join our automotive team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for diagnosing, maintaining, and repairing customer vehicles. You will perform everything from routine maintenance (oil changes and tire rotations) to complex engine and electrical diagnostics. Our goal is to provide high-quality service that ensures vehicle safety and customer satisfaction.

### Key Responsibilities
*   **Diagnostics:** Use diagnostic equipment (such as OBD-II scanners) and conduct test drives to identify mechanical and electrical problems.
*   **Routine Maintenance:** Perform standard maintenance tasks, including oil changes, fluid checks, tire rotations, wheel alignments, and filter replacements.
*   **System Repairs:** Repair and replace parts for various systems, including:
    *   Braking systems (pads, rotors, calipers, ABS).
    *   Suspension and steering components.
    *   Exhaust and fuel systems.
    *   Cooling and heating (HVAC) systems.
*   **Engine & Transmission:** Perform minor and major repairs on engines and transmissions, including timing belts, water pumps, and gaskets.
*   **Electrical Work:** Troubleshoot and repair electrical issues, including batteries, starters, alternators, and wiring harnesses.
*   **Documentation:** Maintain accurate records of all work performed, parts used, and time spent on each job using our shop management software.
*   **Safety & Cleanliness:** Adhere strictly to all safety protocols and maintain a clean, organized workstation and shop environment.

### Required Skills & Qualifications
*   **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent. A degree or certificate from a vocational school or automotive technology program is highly preferred.
*   **Experience:** [Insert Number, e.g., 2-5] years of professional experience as a mechanic.
*   **Certifications:** ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certification is preferred (or a willingness to obtain it).
*   **Technical Knowledge:** Deep understanding of internal combustion engines, electronic systems, and hydraulic systems.
*   **Tool Ownership:** Must own a basic set of hand tools and power tools (specialized equipment will be provided by the shop).
*   **Driving Record:** Valid driver’s license and a clean driving record (required for test-driving vehicles).

### Physical Requirements
*   Ability to stand for long periods (8+ hours).
*   Ability to lift and carry up to 50 lbs.
*   Manual dexterity and the ability to work in tight or awkward spaces.
*   Comfortable working in an environment with noise, vibration, and exposure to chemicals (oil, gas, etc.).

### Soft Skills
*   **Problem-Solving:** Strong analytical skills to troubleshoot complex issues.
*   **Attention to Detail:** Precision is vital for vehicle safety.
*   **Communication:** Ability to explain technical issues to Service Advisors and customers in an easy-to-understand manner.
*   **Time Management:** Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
