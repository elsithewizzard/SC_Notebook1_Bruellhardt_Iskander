### **Job Summary**
We are looking for a detail-oriented and curious Data Analyst to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for turning raw data into actionable insights. You will work closely with stakeholders across the organization to identify business problems, extract and clean data, perform statistical analysis, and create visualizations that drive strategic decision-making.

The ideal candidate has a passion for numbers, a knack for spotting trends, and the ability to communicate complex technical findings to non-technical audiences.

### **Key Responsibilities**
*   **Data Collection & Extraction:** Query and extract data from various sources (SQL databases, APIs, cloud platforms, and flat files).
*   **Data Cleaning & Preparation:** Clean and transform raw data to ensure accuracy, consistency, and readiness for analysis.
*   **Analysis & Modeling:** Use statistical techniques to identify trends, correlations, and patterns in complex data sets.
*   **Reporting & Visualization:** Design and maintain automated dashboards (e.g., Tableau, Power BI) and create clear, concise reports for leadership.
*   **Collaboration:** Partner with department heads (Marketing, Product, Finance, etc.) to understand their data needs and provide analytical support for key initiatives.
*   **Data Integrity:** Monitor data quality and work with the engineering team to troubleshoot any discrepancies or pipeline issues.
*   **Continuous Improvement:** Identify opportunities to improve internal processes and automate manual reporting tasks.

### **Required Skills & Qualifications**
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Economics, or a related field.
*   **Experience:** [X] years of experience in a data analysis or business intelligence role.
*   **SQL:** Expert-level proficiency in SQL (writing complex joins, subqueries, and window functions).
*   **Data Visualization:** Experience building dashboards in **Tableau, Power BI,** or **Looker**.
*   **Spreadsheets:** Advanced Excel skills (VLOOKUPs, Pivot Tables, Macros).
*   **Programming:** Basic to intermediate proficiency in **Python** or **R** (specifically libraries like Pandas, NumPy, or Matplotlib).
*   **Analytical Thinking:** Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to translate business questions into technical queries.
*   **Communication:** Excellent written and verbal communication skills; ability to tell a "story" with data.

### **Preferred Qualifications**
*   Experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift).
*   Knowledge of ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes.
*   Familiarity with machine learning concepts or predictive modeling.
*   Experience in [Specific Industry, e.g., SaaS, E-commerce, or Healthcare].

### **Personal Attributes**
*   **Detail-Oriented:** You notice when a number looks "off" and won't stop until you find out why.
*   **Adaptable:** You thrive in a fast-paced environment and can pivot as business priorities change.
*   **Self-Starter:** You don’t just wait for tasks; you proactively look for ways to use data to help the company.

### **How to Customize This For Your Needs:**
1.  **For a Junior Role:** Focus more on Excel and SQL skills; reduce the required years of experience to 0-2 years.
2.  **For a Senior Role:** Add responsibilities like "Mentoring junior analysts" and "Defining data architecture." Increase the requirement for Python/R and experience with Big Data.
3.  **For a Marketing Analyst:** Add requirements for Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, and Multi-touch Attribution modeling.
4.  **For a Financial Analyst:** Add requirements for P&L reporting, forecasting, and deep knowledge of GAAP or financial modeling.
