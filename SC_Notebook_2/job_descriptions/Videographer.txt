# Job Title: Videographer

**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]
**Job Type:** [Full-time / Part-time / Contract]
**Reports To:** [Creative Director / Marketing Manager]

### Job Summary
We are looking for a creative and technically skilled Videographer to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for leading the end-to-end video production process—from initial concept and storyboarding to filming and post-production editing. The ideal candidate has a keen eye for detail, a strong sense of visual storytelling, and the technical proficiency to handle professional camera gear, lighting, and editing software.

Your goal will be to produce high-quality, engaging video content that aligns with our brand identity and resonates with our target audience across various digital platforms.

### Key Responsibilities
*   **Pre-Production:** Collaborate with the marketing and creative teams to brainstorm concepts, write scripts, and develop storyboards and shot lists.
*   **Production:** Lead on-site and studio film shoots. This includes setting up and operating professional cameras, managing lighting rigs, and ensuring high-quality audio recording.
*   **Post-Production:** Edit raw footage into polished, professional videos using software like Adobe Premiere Pro or Final Cut Pro. This includes color grading, sound design, and adding motion graphics/titles.
*   **Content Strategy:** Adapt video content for various platforms, including YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and our corporate website.
*   **Asset Management:** Organize and archive raw footage and finished projects on company servers or cloud storage.
*   **Equipment Maintenance:** Manage, maintain, and recommend upgrades for all in-house video, audio, and lighting equipment.
*   **Quality Control:** Ensure all video output meets the brand's visual standards and technical requirements.

### Required Skills and Qualifications
*   **Experience:** [X] years of professional experience in video production or a related field.
*   **Portfolio:** A strong demo reel or portfolio showcasing a range of styles (e.g., promotional, documentary, short-form social, corporate).
*   **Technical Proficiency:** Expert knowledge of DSLR, Mirrorless, or Cinema cameras (Sony, Canon, Blackmagic, etc.) and professional audio/lighting gear.
*   **Software Skills:** Mastery of Adobe Creative Cloud (specifically Premiere Pro and After Effects). Proficiency in Photoshop and Audition is a plus.
*   **Storytelling:** A deep understanding of pacing, music selection, and narrative structure to create compelling content.
*   **Time Management:** Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet tight deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
*   **Communication:** Strong interpersonal skills to direct talent on-camera and collaborate with internal stakeholders.

### Preferred Qualifications
*   Degree in Film, Cinematography, Media Studies, or a related field.
*   Experience with drone operation (FAA Part 107 certification is a major plus).
*   Experience with 2D/3D animation or motion graphics.
*   Knowledge of live-streaming setups and software (e.g., OBS, vMix).

### Physical Requirements
*   Ability to lift and carry heavy equipment (up to 50 lbs), including cameras, tripods, and lighting kits.
*   Ability to stand or move for long periods during film shoots.
*   Willingness to travel to various shoot locations as required.

### What We Offer (Optional)
*   Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
*   Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
*   Professional development budget for workshops and equipment training.
*   Creative freedom and a collaborative work environment.
