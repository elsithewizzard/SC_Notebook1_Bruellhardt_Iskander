# Job Title: Videographer

**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]
**Job Type:** [Full-Time / Part-Time / Contract]
**Experience Level:** [Entry, Mid, or Senior Level]

## Job Summary
We are seeking a creative and highly motivated Videographer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for leading the video production process from concept to completion—including storyboarding, filming, and post-production editing. You will work closely with our [Marketing/Creative] team to produce high-quality visual content that tells our brand story, engages our audience, and drives results across various digital platforms.

## Responsibilities
*   **Production:** Plan, film, and direct video shoots in-studio and on-location.
*   **Editing:** Trim footage, sync audio, color grade, and add motion graphics or animations using professional editing software.
*   **Equipment Management:** Set up and operate high-end cameras (DSLR, Mirrorless, or Cinema), professional lighting, and audio recording equipment.
*   **Pre-Production:** Collaborate with stakeholders to develop scripts, storyboards, and shot lists.
*   **Audio/Visual Quality:** Ensure high-quality sound recording and professional lighting for all shoots.
*   **Social Media Optimization:** Create content tailored for various platforms, including YouTube, Instagram (Reels), TikTok, and LinkedIn.
*   **Maintenance:** Maintain and organize all video equipment and manage a structured digital filing system for all raw and finished footage.
*   **Stay Current:** Keep up-to-date with the latest trends in video technology, editing techniques, and social media formats.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
*   **Portfolio:** A strong demo reel or portfolio showcasing a variety of video projects.
*   **Software Proficiency:** Expert knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (Premiere Pro and After Effects are a must; Photoshop and Illustrator are a plus) or Final Cut Pro/DaVinci Resolve.
*   **Technical Knowledge:** Deep understanding of framing, lighting, composition, and sound design.
*   **Communication:** Excellent verbal and written communication skills for collaborating with team members and directing talent.
*   **Time Management:** Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet tight deadlines.
*   **Physical Stamina:** Ability to carry heavy equipment (up to 40 lbs) and stand for long periods during shoots.

## Education & Experience
*   [X] years of professional experience in video production or a related field.
*   Degree in Film, Media Studies, Cinematography, or equivalent experience/certifications.

## Preferred Qualifications (Nice-to-Haves)
*   Experience with drone operation and FAA Part 107 certification.
*   Advanced motion graphics and 3D animation skills.
*   Experience in live-streaming setups (OBS, Blackmagic Atem, etc.).
*   Knowledge of photography and photo editing.

### Pro-Tips for the Hiring Manager:
1.  **Be specific about the gear:** If your studio uses specific cameras (e.g., RED, Sony Alpha, or Blackmagic), mention it so the candidate knows if they need to be familiar with that ecosystem.
2.  **Define the output:** Is the videographer making 30-second TikToks or 10-minute documentaries? Clarity here helps attract the right specialist.
3.  **The "Reel" is everything:** In this field, the portfolio is more important than the resume. Prioritize candidates who show a style that matches your brand vision.
