### **Job Title: Author / Staff Writer**

**Reports to:** Editorial Director, Lead Editor, or Creative Manager
**Location:** [Remote/On-site/Hybrid]
**Employment Type:** [Full-time/Part-time/Contract]

---

### **Job Summary**
We are seeking a creative, disciplined, and versatile Author to produce high-quality written content. The ideal candidate will be responsible for conceptualizing, researching, and writing original manuscripts, articles, or books that resonate with our target audience. Whether crafting compelling narratives or translating complex information into accessible prose, the Author will play a pivotal role in shaping our brand voice and expanding our literary or commercial reach.

### **Key Responsibilities**
*   **Content Creation:** Draft, write, and polish original manuscripts (fiction or non-fiction), articles, essays, or scripts according to established guidelines and deadlines.
*   **Research:** Conduct thorough research on specific topics, historical periods, or technical data to ensure accuracy and depth in storytelling or reporting.
*   **Conceptualization:** Brainstorm and develop unique story ideas, themes, characters, or educational concepts that align with market trends or organizational goals.
*   **Collaboration:** Work closely with editors, proofreaders, and creative directors to incorporate feedback, refine drafts, and ensure the final product meets quality standards.
*   **Revision:** Perform multiple rounds of self-editing and structural revisions to improve clarity, pacing, tone, and grammar.
*   **Market Awareness:** Stay updated on industry trends, competitor works, and audience preferences to ensure content remains relevant and engaging.
*   **Promotion:** Assist the marketing and PR teams in creating promotional materials, attending book signings, participating in interviews, or engaging with readers on social media.
*   **Project Management:** Manage multiple writing projects simultaneously, ensuring all milestones and publication deadlines are met.

### **Required Skills and Qualifications**
*   **Exceptional Writing Ability:** Mastery of the English language, including a deep understanding of grammar, syntax, style, and storytelling techniques.
*   **Creativity:** A proven ability to generate original ideas and tell compelling stories that captivate an audience.
*   **Research Proficiency:** Strong skills in identifying credible sources and distilling complex information.
*   **Discipline and Time Management:** The ability to work independently, maintain focus during long periods of writing, and consistently meet strict deadlines.
*   **Adaptability:** Ability to shift tone and style based on the specific project or target demographic.
*   **Communication:** Strong interpersonal skills for collaborating with publishing teams and responding constructively to critiques.

### **Education and Experience**
*   **Education:** A Bachelor’s degree in English, Creative Writing, Journalism, Communications, or a related field is preferred (though significant talent/experience can often substitute for a degree).
*   **Experience:** [X] years of professional writing experience.
*   **Portfolio:** A strong portfolio of published work (books, articles, or a blog) or a high-quality manuscript sample.
*   **Technical Skills:** Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Scrivener, or other writing and formatting software. Familiarity with Content Management Systems (CMS) is a plus.

### **Preferred Qualifications**
*   Experience with the traditional or self-publishing process.
*   An established platform or following in a specific niche (e.g., Sci-Fi, True Crime, Technical Manuals, or Self-Help).
*   Basic understanding of SEO (Search Engine Optimization) for digital authors.

### **Work Environment**
*   Most of the work is performed in a quiet, self-directed environment.
*   Occasional travel may be required for research, book tours, or industry conferences.
