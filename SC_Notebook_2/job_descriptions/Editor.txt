# Job Title: Editor

**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]
**Job Type:** [Full-time / Part-time / Contract]
**Experience Level:** [Entry, Mid, or Senior Level]

### **Job Summary**
We are seeking a meticulous, creative, and highly organized Editor to join our content team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for reviewing, revising, and refining content across various platforms to ensure it meets our high standards of quality, accuracy, and brand voice. You will work closely with writers, designers, and marketing teams to transform raw drafts into polished, engaging, and error-free pieces of work.

### **Key Responsibilities**
*   **Content Refining:** Review and edit content for errors in grammar, punctuation, spelling, and syntax while improving overall flow and readability.
*   **Brand Consistency:** Ensure all content aligns with the company’s brand voice, tone, and internal style guides.
*   **Fact-Checking:** Verify the accuracy of facts, dates, statistics, and citations to maintain the integrity of our publication.
*   **Content Strategy:** Collaborate with the editorial team to brainstorm new content ideas, manage editorial calendars, and meet publication deadlines.
*   **SEO Optimization:** Apply SEO best practices to digital content (headlines, meta descriptions, and keywords) to increase organic reach and engagement.
*   **Writer Management:** Provide constructive feedback to staff writers and freelance contributors to help them improve their craft and adhere to guidelines.
*   **Formatting:** Prepare and format content in Content Management Systems (CMS), such as WordPress, including the addition of images, links, and tags.
*   **Legal Compliance:** Ensure content does not violate copyright laws or include libelous material.

### **Required Skills and Qualifications**
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in English, Journalism, Communications, or a related field.
*   **Experience:** [Number] years of experience in editing, professional writing, or publishing.
*   **Language Mastery:** Exceptional command of the English language, including nuances in tone and style.
*   **Style Guide Proficiency:** Deep familiarity with major style guides (e.g., AP Stylebook, Chicago Manual of Style, or MLA).
*   **Attention to Detail:** An eagle eye for typos and the ability to spot inconsistencies that others might miss.
*   **Software Skills:** Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, and experience using a CMS (WordPress, Drupal, etc.).
*   **Time Management:** Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously under tight deadlines.

### **Preferred Qualifications**
*   Experience with SEO tools (e.g., Semrush, Ahrefs, or Yoast).
*   Basic knowledge of HTML and CSS.
*   Graphic design basics (Canva or Adobe Creative Suite) for selecting or editing visual aids.
*   Experience in a specific niche relevant to our company (e.g., Tech, Finance, Lifestyle).

### **Personal Attributes**
*   **Collaborative Spirit:** Comfortable working in a team environment and receiving/giving feedback.
*   **Critical Thinker:** Able to evaluate the logic and structure of an argument or story.
*   **Adaptable:** Quick to pivot when priorities shift or when a new brand voice is introduced.
