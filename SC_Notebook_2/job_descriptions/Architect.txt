### **Job Summary**
We are seeking a creative, detail-oriented, and technically proficient Architect to join our design team. In this role, you will be responsible for leading projects from the initial conceptual stage through to completion. You will translate client visions into functional, safe, and aesthetically pleasing structures while ensuring all designs comply with zoning laws and building codes. The ideal candidate has a strong portfolio, excellent project management skills, and a mastery of architectural software.

### **Key Responsibilities**
*   **Design Development:** Create detailed blueprints, 3D models, and construction documents using BIM/CAD software. 
*   **Project Management:** Manage project lifecycles, including budgets, schedules, and resource allocation.
*   **Client Collaboration:** Meet with clients to discuss project briefs, budgets, and design preferences; present proposals and adjustments as needed.
*   **Compliance & Permitting:** Ensure all designs adhere to local building codes, ADA requirements, zoning laws, and environmental regulations.
*   **Technical Coordination:** Work closely with structural engineers, electrical engineers, and MEP consultants to integrate building systems into the design.
*   **Construction Administration:** Conduct site visits to monitor progress, ensure work aligns with architectural plans, and resolve any design issues that arise during construction.
*   **Sustainability:** Research and incorporate sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems into designs where possible.

### **Required Skills & Qualifications**
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Architecture from an accredited institution.
*   **Licensure:** Active Architect license in [State/Region] (NCARB certification preferred).
*   **Technical Proficiency:** 
    *   Expertise in **Revit** (required) and **AutoCAD**.
    *   Experience with 3D modeling and rendering software (e.g., Rhino, SketchUp, Lumion, or V-Ray).
    *   Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign).
*   **Design Sense:** Strong aesthetic vision and a deep understanding of spatial relationships, materials, and light.
*   **Communication:** Excellent verbal and visual communication skills for client presentations and team collaboration.
*   **Problem-Solving:** Ability to solve complex technical challenges and adapt designs to unforeseen site conditions.
