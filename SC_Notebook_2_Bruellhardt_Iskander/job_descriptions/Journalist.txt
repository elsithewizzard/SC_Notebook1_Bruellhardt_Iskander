# Job Title: Journalist / News Reporter

**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]
**Job Type:** [Full-Time / Part-Time / Contract]
**Reports to:** [Editor-in-Chief / Managing Editor]

### Job Summary
We are seeking a curious, tenacious, and principled Journalist to join our newsroom. The ideal candidate is a storyteller at heart with a passion for uncovering the truth and a knack for explaining complex issues to a diverse audience. You will be responsible for pitching, researching, and writing compelling stories that range from breaking news to in-depth investigative features.

### Key Responsibilities
*   **Story Sourcing:** Proactively identify, pitch, and pursue original story ideas that align with our audience’s interests and the organization’s editorial mission.
*   **Reporting & Research:** Conduct thorough research, verify facts, and interview key sources (including officials, experts, and community members) to ensure accuracy and depth.
*   **Writing & Content Creation:** Write clear, engaging, and unbiased articles, scripts, or blog posts under tight deadlines.
*   **Multimedia Integration:** Collaborate with photographers and videographers to enhance stories; capture basic photos or video when necessary.
*   **Social Media & Engagement:** Utilize social media platforms to promote published work, engage with readers, and monitor emerging trends or news tips.
*   **Networking:** Build and maintain a reliable network of sources and contacts within specific beats (e.g., local government, tech, sports, or crime).
*   **Fact-Checking & Ethics:** Adhere strictly to journalistic ethics, legal guidelines (libel/defamation laws), and the organization's style guide (e.g., AP Style).

### Required Skills and Qualifications
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Journalism, Communications, English, or a related field.
*   **Experience:** [X] years of experience in a newsroom or professional writing environment.
*   **Portfolio:** A strong portfolio of published clips demonstrating versatility and high-quality reporting.
*   **Writing Excellence:** Exceptional command of grammar, tone, and storytelling techniques.
*   **Technical Proficiency:** Familiarity with Content Management Systems (CMS) like WordPress, SEO best practices, and social media analytics.
*   **Interpersonal Skills:** Strong interviewing skills and the ability to build rapport with diverse groups of people.

### Preferred Qualifications
*   Experience with data journalism or investigative reporting.
*   Proficiency in a second language (e.g., Spanish, Mandarin).
*   Basic skills in photo/video editing software (Adobe Creative Suite, etc.).
*   Knowledge of a specific niche or "beat."

### Key Competencies
*   **Adaptability:** Ability to pivot quickly when news breaks or stories change.
*   **Integrity:** Unwavering commitment to objectivity and truth-telling.
*   **Resilience:** Ability to handle rejection of pitches and work effectively under high-pressure deadlines.
*   **Critical Thinking:** The capacity to look beyond the surface of a press release or statement to find the real story.
