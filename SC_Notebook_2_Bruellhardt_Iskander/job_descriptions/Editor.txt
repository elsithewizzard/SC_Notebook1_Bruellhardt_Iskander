# Job Title: Editor

**Location:** [City, State / Remote / Hybrid]  
**Reports to:** [e.g., Content Manager, Creative Director, or Editor-in-Chief]  
**Job Type:** [Full-time / Part-time / Contract]

### Job Summary
We are seeking a meticulous and creative Editor to oversee our content production and ensure all written materials are of the highest quality. The ideal candidate will have a sharp eye for detail, a mastery of language, and the ability to coach writers to reach their full potential. You will be responsible for everything from proofreading for grammar and style to shaping the structural flow of long-form pieces, all while maintaining our brand’s unique voice.

### Key Responsibilities
*   **Content Review:** Edit, proofread, and rewrite content for clarity, accuracy, grammar, and tone.
*   **Brand Consistency:** Ensure all content adheres to the company’s style guide and maintains a consistent brand voice across all channels.
*   **Fact-Checking:** Verify dates, names, statistics, and facts to ensure the credibility of every piece of content.
*   **Writer Management:** Assign stories to internal writers or freelancers, provide constructive feedback, and manage deadlines.
*   **SEO Optimization:** (For Digital Roles) Collaborate with the SEO team to ensure content is optimized for search engines while remaining engaging for human readers.
*   **Content Strategy:** Participate in brainstorming sessions to develop new content ideas, themes, and editorial calendars.
*   **Project Management:** Oversee the production lifecycle of a piece, from initial pitch to final publication.
*   **Collaboration:** Work closely with designers, marketing teams, and subject matter experts to integrate visuals and data into written content.

### Required Skills & Qualifications
*   **Experience:** 3–5 years of experience in editing, journalism, or professional writing.
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in English, Journalism, Communications, or a related field.
*   **Grammar Mastery:** Exceptional command of the English language, including a deep understanding of syntax and punctuation.
*   **Style Guide Proficiency:** Expertise in one or more major style guides (e.g., AP Style, Chicago Manual of Style, or MLA).
*   **Attention to Detail:** An uncanny ability to spot typos and factual inconsistencies that others miss.
*   **Communication:** Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to provide critical feedback in a positive, professional manner.
*   **Adaptability:** Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple projects simultaneously under tight deadlines.

### Preferred Qualifications
*   **CMS Experience:** Familiarity with Content Management Systems like WordPress, Contentful, or Ghost.
*   **Technical Knowledge:** Basic understanding of HTML/CSS and SEO best practices.
*   **Niche Expertise:** [Insert specific industry knowledge here, e.g., "Experience in B2B SaaS," "Fashion journalism background," or "Technical editing experience."]
*   **Visual Eye:** Experience with basic photo editing or layout design tools (e.g., Canva, Adobe Creative Suite).

### How to Tailor This for Your Specific Needs:
*   **If hiring a Copy Editor:** Focus more on the "Technical Skills" section, emphasizing grammar, fact-checking, and style guides.
