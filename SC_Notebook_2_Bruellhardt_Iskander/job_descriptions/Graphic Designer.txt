## **Job Summary**
We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented Graphic Designer to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for creating high-quality visual content that communicates our brand message across digital and print platforms. The ideal candidate is a visual storyteller who can take a project from concept to completion, ensuring brand consistency and aesthetic excellence at every step.

## **Key Responsibilities**
*   **Visual Content Creation:** Design original pieces, including illustrations, infographics, social media graphics, website banners, and marketing collateral (brochures, decks, and white papers).
*   **Branding:** Assist in the development and maintenance of brand identity guidelines, ensuring all materials align with our visual voice.
*   **Collaboration:** Work closely with the marketing, product, and sales teams to understand project requirements and provide creative solutions that meet business goals.
*   **Layout & Typography:** Create professional layouts with a strong eye for typography, hierarchy, and color theory.
*   **Project Management:** Manage multiple projects simultaneously, meeting deadlines in a fast-paced environment while maintaining high quality.
*   **Production:** Prepare final files for print and digital production, ensuring all technical specifications (color profiles, bleeds, resolutions) are met.
*   **Iteration:** Receive and apply constructive feedback from stakeholders to refine designs through several rounds of revisions.

## **Required Skills & Qualifications**
*   **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, Fine Arts, or a related field (or equivalent professional experience).
*   **Software Expertise:** Advanced proficiency in Adobe Creative Cloud (specifically **Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign**).
*   **Portfolio:** A strong portfolio demonstrating a wide range of design skills, including layout, branding, and digital design.
*   **Creativity:** A keen eye for aesthetics, detail, and current design trends.
*   **Communication:** Excellent verbal and written communication skills to articulate design choices to non-designers.
*   **Time Management:** Proven ability to prioritize tasks and deliver work on schedule.

## **Preferred Qualifications (Nice-to-Haves)**
*   Experience with motion graphics and video editing (**After Effects, Premiere Pro**).
*   Knowledge of UI/UX design principles and tools like **Figma or Adobe XD**.
*   Basic understanding of HTML/CSS or experience with CMS platforms like WordPress.
*   Experience in photography or photo art direction.
